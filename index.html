work rules

A few rules for better workplace

make sure your following this rules

  • Everyone wants to feel like what they do matters
  • Trust your employees to behave like a founder and create an ethos of ownership in the team
  • Give people slightly more trust, freedom and authority than you give them now
  • Be transparent and share information
  • Learn as much as possible from your top performers
  • Slow down and invest time upfront to hire the best people
  • If you help those who struggle most to improve substantially, then you have created a cycle of continuous improvement
